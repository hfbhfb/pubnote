2, cordova会走android的逻辑的gradle

3, 通过迅雷下载好对应的版本 https://downloads.gradle-dn.com/distributions/gradle-4.10.3-all.zip

4, 把对应版本拷贝到相应的目录 /Users/hfb/.gradle/wrapper/dists/gradle-4.10.3-all/81msde2dx9p4vji0mjgtvxkcb
:::
